WELCOME TO OUR WEBSITE

UNIQUE STEEL TUBES (sister concern of M/S PERFECT PIPES PVT LTD, DELHI, since 1990) became operational in 2018. UST is Delhi based having its manufacturing units at Swarn Park, Udhyog Nagar. Mundka. We have two Precision Tube Mills, ½’’ to 1.5’’ Od and 5/8’’ to 4’’ Od. We also have a slitting machine for slitting upto 1600 mm wide coils. We make all shapes of pipes/ tubes like Round, Square, Rectangle, D, Triangle, Capsule/ Elliptical etc. We manufacture in all finishes like CR. HR, Hrop, GP and Gpsp in Mild Steel. We manufacture Pipes/Tubes from 0.30mm to 3.0mm thickness. We cater to many industries like Automobile, Furniture, Heat Exchangers and allied engineering industries. We also have a well equipped in house Tool Room for new developments and repairs.


We also deal in sale and purchase of Brand new as well as Used Tube Mill & Machinery.
